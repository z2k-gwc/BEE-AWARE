Step 1: Make sure that you are dealing with bees.
- It is important to make the distinction between bees and wasps or hornets the former contributes to pollinating plants, flowers, and our food source, while the latter are just pests. Wasps and hornets are also agressive so it is important to be aware of which insect you are dealing with.
Step 2: Call a local beekeeper or bee remmoval company
- Using local beekeepers is usually more preferable to calling exterminators. Bees are important to our environments so depite the added difficulty of removing bees without killing them it is definitely worth it. We have provided a list of local Houston bee removal specialists.
