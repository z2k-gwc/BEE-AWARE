What about Honey makes it special?

The clear, golden amber liquid is sweet and made by hardworking bees using the nectar from flowers.

Health Benefits of Honey

Antiseptics

1. Honey contains antiseptics that have been proven to reduce stroke risk.

Helps Infections

2. Honey has antibacterical properties and modern medical science have managed to find use for
honey in chronic wound managment and combating infection. The Mayo Clinic says that honey can sterilize wounds and
promote healing, and also reduce pain, odor, and wound size.
It can also treat antibiotic-resistant bacteria and long-term
ulcers and wounds after surgery and from burns.

Heart

3. Honey is healthy for your heart and can protect you from heart disease with improving cholesterol levels.

Fertility

4. Honey has been hyped for its potential to boost fertility in both men and women.

Psoriasis

5. Psoriasis is a common uncurable skin condition that causes redness, blistering, itching. Study has shown that using a mixture of honey, olive oil and beeswax has helped reduction of redness and more for patients.

Blood Sugar regulation

6. With a low glycemic index, it’s a good substitute for sugar and can help you monitor blood sugar

Intresting Facts about Honey



1. A typical beehive can produce anywhere from 30 to 100 pounds of honey a year.
To produce a single pound of honey, a colony of bees must collect nectar from approximately
two million flowers and fly over 55,000 miles. This amounts to a lifetime’s worth of work for around 800 bees.
2. In 11th century Germany, honey was so highly valued for its beer-sweetening abilities that
German feudal lords required their peasants to make them payments of honey and beeswax.
3. Bees work hard all summer to ensure they’ll have enough honey
to sustain the hive through the winter. During the colder months, bees occupy their time by clustering themselves
around the queen and shivering their bodies to fill the hive with warmth. All that shivering burns a lot of calories, so honey makes for the perfect high-energy diet.
