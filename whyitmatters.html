Why Plants Need Pollination 

     Plants need pollinators for a number of
    reasons.Pollinating animals travel from
    plant to plant carrying pollen on their bodies
    for a vital interaction.This interaction allows
    the transfer of genetic material crucial to the
    reproductive systems of most flowering plants.
    Pollinators are responsible for providing 1 out
    of 3 bites of food.
